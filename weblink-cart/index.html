Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)

Q. What Is WeblinkCart?

WeblinkCart is a ready-made ecommerce website solution that helps you launch your online store quickly and affordably. We provide complete ecommerce packages starting from INR 8,999 + GST, including design, setup, and essential ecommerce features..

Q. Do you offer a free store?

No, we do not offer a free store. Our most affordable package starts at INR 8,999 + GST, which includes a ready-made ecommerce website with essential features, domain, hosting, and basic support.

Q. What do I get with the INR 8,999 package?

Our Economy Package includes :

  • Ready-made ecommerce website design
  • Product catalog setup (limited number of products)
  • Payment gateway integration
  • Mobile-friendly design
  • Admin panel to manage products & orders
  • Complimentary domain (for the first year)
  • Free hosting (for the first year)
  • Basic support & training

Q. Can I customize my ecommerce website?

Our ready-made solutions come with pre-designed layouts to keep costs low. Limited customization is possible, such as logo updates, banner changes, and basic color adjustments. For full customization, please connect with our sales team for a tailored quote.

Q. Do I need any technical skills to run my store?

No, you don’t! WeblinkCart is designed for everyone — no coding knowledge needed. You’ll get an easy-to-use admin panel to manage products, orders, and customers.

Q. Can I upgrade my package later?

Yes, you can easily upgrade to a higher package or add more features as your business grows. Our team will guide you through the process.

Q. What payment gateways are supported?

We integrate popular payment gateways such as Razorpay, Phonepe, CCAvenue, and more. We can suggest the best option based on your location and business type.

Q. How long does it take to launch my store?

Once we receive all the required details from you (products, logo, content), your store can be live within **7–10 working days**, depending on the package.

Q. Is there any hidden cost?

No hidden costs. You only pay the package price plus GST. Renewal charges for domain & hosting will apply from the second year onwards.

Q. Do you provide support?

Yes, we provide basic support through calls, email, and WhatsApp during working hours. Our team is here to help you run your store smoothly..

Q. Can I see a demo?

Absolutely! Please visit weblinkcart to view our demo stores or contact our sales team for a personalized walkthrough..
