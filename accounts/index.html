Billing System

  • Home
  • Billing System

This section is to ensure that our customers have full access to their payment details. To strengthen and add transparency to our customer relationship management system, we keep a well-maintained database of all the payments that our customers have made till date on the following portals:

  • ExportersIndia.com
  • RealEstateIndia.com
  • PlacementIndia.com
  • TourTravelWorld.com
  • MatrimonialsIndia.com
  • WeblinkIndia.Net

Simply fill in your login details (username and password) and click on the ‘Login’ button below to get a complete history of the payments you have made so far. You can also access the payment bill details from the portal, where you have membership, as well.

Once you have logged in, you can:

  • View your opening and closing balance
  • View the date-wise statement of each financial year
  • Check and print our your Bills

Note:

  • All the details of the payments that have been processed on/after 1st April 2012 would be available here.
  • All disputes are subject to Delhi Jurisdiction only.
