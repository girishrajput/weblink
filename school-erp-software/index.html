Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)

Q. What is the Utility of an Online School Management Software?

The School ERP Software doubles as a central dashboard where all the stakeholders including parents, teachers, management and principals could access all the important details anytime they want, even on the go. Through this online school management software, the teachers can dedicate their time exclusively to lesson planning and keeping track of students’ performance. It is a great way through which the parents can keep themselves updated with their child’s performance in the class. The management on the other hand can manage the time-consuming administrative jobs, whereas the principals can keep track of the actions carried out inside the school premises.

Q. What Does the Best ERP School Software like Edu Automate Contain?

  • Administrative Area
  • Academic Area
  • Communication Area
  • Financial Area

Q. How is School Management System Beneficial?

Through its e-learning platform, it can empower both students and teachers.

  • It keeps the teachers, and students connected at all times through mobile apps, email and SMS.
  • It saves paper and digitizes both administrative and non-administrative tasks.
  • It helps track students on their go and even during to-and-fro from home.
  • Attractive website presence to portray your school’s educational vision and mission.
  • The online admission system enables better and improved student enrolment.

Q. What Features to Look for in Best School ERP Software in India?

  • Multiple-User Functionality
  • User-Friendly Interface
  • Customizable Modules
  • Data Security and Backup
The school software ERP is designed for effective management of administrative and non-administrative school activities like daily activities monitoring, and role-based user access to restrict leakage of sensitive institutional information. The School ERP Software is trusted by various schools.
