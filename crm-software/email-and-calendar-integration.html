Email and Calendar Integration

Centralise Communication and Scheduling for Maximum Efficiency
Home / CRM Software / Email And Calendar Integration

CRMManaging client relationships requires constant communication and precise scheduling. CRM@Weblink bridges the gap between your inbox, your calendar, and your customer database. This integration removes the need to switch between multiple tabs or applications to find a client’s history or book a meeting. You see every email exchange and upcoming appointment right next to the client's profile. This immediate access to information empowers your team to respond faster and make informed decisions without hunting for scattered data.

The system creates a two-way link between your preferred email provider and the CRM dashboard. When you send a message from the CRM, it appears in your sent folder. Conversely, incoming client emails log automatically under their specific contact record. Calendar events work similarly; an appointment booked in the CRM reflects instantly on your personal calendar. This synchronisation ensures that your entire team stays aligned. No one misses a meeting or overlooks a critical email because the data lives in one central, accessible location. By consolidating these tools, you reduce administrative friction and focus entirely on building stronger client connections.

Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ's)

Q. Does the integration work with both Google and Microsoft Outlook calendars?
Yes, CRM@Weblink supports seamless integration with major providers, including Google Calendar and Microsoft Outlook. You can connect your preferred account during the setup process, and the system will handle the synchronisation for both emails and schedule entries
Q. Will my private personal appointments be visible to the entire sales team?
No, you control the privacy settings. While the system checks your calendar for availability to prevent double-booking, you can mark specific events as private. This ensures that personal appointments remain confidential while still blocking out that time in your schedule
Q. Does the system sync emails that I sent before I installed the CRM?
The synchronisation primarily focuses on real-time and future activities. However, depending on your specific configuration settings, you may choose to import a limited history of past emails for contacts that already exist within your database
Q. Can I use the calendar integration to book meetings for other team members?
Yes, if you have the appropriate administrative permissions. You can view the availability of your colleagues and schedule meetings on their behalf, which is particularly useful for receptionists or sales development representatives booking appointments for account managers
Q. What happens if I change a meeting time in my phone's calendar app?
The two-way sync ensures that any change you make on your phone's native calendar app updates the CRM immediately. The meeting time will adjust on the client's profile, and the system will maintain accuracy without manual input
Q. Is it possible to send bulk emails through this integration?
The primary purpose of this integration is one-on-one transactional communication. For mass mailing or marketing campaigns, we recommend using the dedicated marketing tools within CRM@Weblink, which are designed to handle bulk sending and compliance
