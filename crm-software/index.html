Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ's)

Q. How do I add a new contact to the CRM?
To add a new contact, simply navigate to the Customer section in the CRM dashboard and click on the "New Customers or Add (in View Customers)" button. Then, fill in the required details such as name, email, phone number, and any other relevant information. Click "Save" to add the contact to your database.
Q. Can I access the CRM from my Mobile device/Application?
Yes, our CRM software is fully mobile-responsive, allowing you to access it from any device with an internet connection. Simply log in to your account using your mobile browser or download our mobile app for optimized access on the go.
Q. How do I track my sales pipeline in the CRM?
Tracking your sales pipeline is easy with our CRM software. Navigate to the Deal section and view the pipeline dashboard, where you can see the status of each deal, from prospecting to closure by shiffting them.
Q. Can I integrate the CRM with other tools and software we use?
Yes, our CRM software offers seamless integration with a wide range of popular tools and software, including email clients, marketing automation platforms, and accounting software. Simply navigate to the Integrations section in the CRM settings to connect with your preferred tools.
Q. How can I generate reports to track performance in the CRM?
Generating reports is straightforward with our CRM software. Navigate to the Reports section and select the type of report you'd like to generate, such as sales performance, customer engagement, or lead conversion. Customize the parameters and filters, then generate the report to gain valuable insights into your business metrics.
