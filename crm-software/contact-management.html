Customer & Contact Management

Build stronger customer relationships with a complete customer management system
Home / CRM Software / Contact Management

CRM@Weblink is a customer and contact management system designed to help businesses manage complete customer information from a single platform. It captures every customer interaction including calls, emails, meetings, notes, and follow-ups so teams always have the full context.

This customer management CRM provides a 360-degree view of each customer, helping sales, support, and marketing teams work together efficiently. From first enquiry to repeat business, customer data remains organised, updated, and accessible in real time.

Built for Indian businesses, CRM@Weblink eliminates spreadsheets and manual tracking. With structured customer records, smart segmentation, and activity timelines, businesses can close deals faster, improve customer satisfaction, and build long-term customer loyalty.

Key Benefits of Contact Management

Manage customers efficiently, improve engagement, and increase retention from one unified CRM system

Centralised Customer Database
Maintain a single, secure database for all customers including contact details, communication history, and business interactions.
Complete Customer Interaction Tracking
Track every customer call, email, meeting, and follow-up in one timeline to ensure consistent and informed communication.
Customer Segmentation & Classification
Segment customers based on location, industry, buying stage, or engagement level for targeted and relevant communication.
Customer Follow-Up & Reminder System
Automated reminders ensure timely customer follow-ups and reduce missed sales or support opportunities.
Secure Customer Data Access
Role-based access ensures customer data is visible only to authorised team members, maintaining data security and privacy.
Mobile-Friendly Customer Management
Access and manage customer information anytime through a fast, mobile-optimised CRM interface.

How the Contact Management System work?

Each customer follows a structured journey from enquiry to long-term relationship

Capture Customer Enquiries
Customer details are captured automatically from website forms, incoming calls, emails, or manual entry.
Assign Customers to Teams
Customers are automatically assigned to the appropriate sales or support team based on predefined rules.
Track Customer Communication
All communication history is logged under each customer profile to maintain continuity and transparency.
Customer Scoring & Priority
Customers are prioritised based on interest level, engagement history, or business potential.
Add Customer Notes & Tags
Internal notes and tags help teams personalise future interactions and understand customer preferences.
Sync Customer Data Across Devices
Customer updates are synced instantly across desktop and mobile devices.
Export Customer Records Easily
Customer data can be filtered and exported in Excel format for reporting or analysis.
Bulk Customer Updates
Update multiple customer records at once using bulk actions to save time and effort.
Archive Inactive Customers
Inactive customers can be archived without losing historical data and reactivated anytime when required.

Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ's)

Q. How can I import my existing customers into CRM@Weblink?
Customers can be imported in bulk using Excel or CSV files. The system automatically maps fields and removes duplicates
Q. Can multiple teams manage the same customer?
Yes. Sales, support, and management teams can collaborate on the same customer record using role-based access
Q. Does the system remind me about customer follow-ups?
Yes. You can set manual or recurring reminders for each customer and receive alerts via email or system notifications
Q. Is customer data secure in the CRM?
All customer data is stored securely with SSL encryption and role-level access control
Q. Can I export my customer database?
Yes. You can export selected or complete customer records in Excel format at any time
Q. Do you provide onboarding support?
Yes. The support team assists with customer data import, system setup, team training, and customisation
